    Year round BOOK Lessons All surf lesson packages include the following: Surf instructions and hands on guidance with Clint Carroll and his experienced team of world-class surf instructors. We will have beginners surfing on their first day! If it's been a while, we'll RECONNECT your surfing skills! All equipment is provided, including: surfboard, wetsuit & leash The Surf School is conveniently located only one block from the beach in world famous Huntington Beach, California (see map) Transportation is available upon request: Clint Carroll Surf School will provide shuttle service at your hotel 1/2 hour prior to your scheduled lesson. Please bring swim wear, towel, and sun block. Upon request, we also book early mornings as well as private parties, private groups, corporate events, special needs, you name it! Please call for a special rate for groups 5 or more in size. Pricing packages are 3 days / 2 nights for $1500 or 5 days / 4 nights for $2500, you can add 3 surf lessons to your package for $250. Please call 949-355-7285 to schedule the following: booking your lesson 24 hours (or less) prior to the lesson start time special times and special treatments - available upon request
